What a live pilot actually looks like

No magic and no black box. Here's exactly how this demo becomes real texts to your real customers — and what stays in your control.

  1. 1 · We connect a texting line

    We set up a business SMS number through a regulated provider (Twilio). It can text from your existing number's identity, or use a fresh local line — your call.

  2. 2 · Missed calls trigger the text-back

    When a call to your line goes unanswered, the same conversation you just tested fires automatically. Nothing changes about how you answer calls you can take.

  3. 3 · You approve the wording

    Before anything goes live, you sign off on the greeting, the questions, and where confirmed leads get sent (your phone, email, or a shared inbox).

  4. 4 · Leads land where you work

    Each confirmed lead shows on the dashboard and can be forwarded to you instantly. You stay in the loop; sensitive or unusual requests route straight to you.

Honest about limits: this is the missed-call intake piece — it captures and confirms leads. It doesn't take payments or manage your calendar (yet). We start small, prove it recovers jobs you'd have lost, then decide what's next together.
